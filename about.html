About Us

I am currently a student at Doral Academy Preparatory High School, where I am deeply involved in academic and extracurricular pursuits that center around technology, learning, and helping others grow.

Pathfinder was created as a solution to help students better understand the alignment between their current coursework and their future goals—whether that’s college admissions, career preparation, or skill-building.

My mission is to continue improving the Pathfinder platform, integrating advanced AI features and personalized tracking tools to make education more strategic, accessible, and impactful.
